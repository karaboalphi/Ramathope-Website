About Us

Ramathope High School is a community-focused school dedicated to learner achievement, discipline and community service. We aim to develop knowledgeable, skilled and values-driven young people who will lead our community into the future.

Academics

Subjects

  • Mathematics & Mathematical Literacy
  • Physical Sciences & Life Sciences
  • English Home Language & First Additional Language
  • Geography, History & Life Orientation
  • Accounting, Business Studies & Computer Applications

Extra-curricular

Sports, choir, debate, science club and community outreach programmes to develop learners beyond the classroom.

Resources

Computer lab, library, science laboratory and qualified teachers committed to learner support and academic growth.
